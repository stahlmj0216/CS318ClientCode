OUR MISSION

The mission of the American Museum of True Crime is to educate and bring awareness to true crime in the United States while bringing remembrance to those who have passedand acknowledgingsurvivors. The institution provides an opportunity for the mature audience to both learn and be investigative. Exhibits include serial killers, cold cases, unsolved mysteries, and white collar crime. The museum also features a gift shop.
